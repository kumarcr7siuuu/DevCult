# Instagram Content Templates for Silver Cosmic

Since you're building a high-tech/design brand, your "templates" should be consistent layouts that you repeat. This builds brand recognition.

## 1. The "Work Showcase" (For sharing your projects)
**Goal:** Show off your design/coding skills.
**Visual Style:** Clean device frame (laptop or phone) on a blurred, abstract background.

*   **Slide 1 (Hook):** High-res screenshot of the project with a bold title overlay.

## 3. The "Educational Carousel" (Value)
**Goal:** Teach your audience something (saves/shares).
**Visual Style:** Dark mode card with glowing borders.

*   **Card 1:** Title: "3 Tools I use for [Task]"
*   **Card 2:** Tool 1 + Screenshot + Why you love it.
*   **Card 3:** Tool 2 + Screenshot + Why you love it.
*   **Card 4:** Tool 3 + Screenshot + Why you love it.

## 4. The "Thought Leader" (Minimalist)
**Goal:** Share an opinion or update.
**Visual Style:** Solid dark background (Hex: #0a0a0a), large Serif font (white), small logo at bottom.

*   **Content:**
    *   "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."
    *   "Just shipped the MVP for Silver Cosmic. Here's what I learned..."

## Free Tools to Create These:
1.  **Canva:** Search for "Dark Mode Carousel" or "Tech Instagram Post".
2.  **Figma:** Best for custom layouts. You can create a "Master Component" for your frames.
3.  **Shots.so (Highly Recommended):** Free tool to framing your screenshots in nice device mockups. Perfect for Template #1.
